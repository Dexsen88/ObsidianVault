**Incident Response (IR)** is the mandatory, structured process an organization follows to prepare for, detect, contain, analyze, and recover from a security breach or cyberattack.

- **Goal:** To minimize the damage, cost, and recovery time of a security incident. The process relies heavily on secure **[[Security Logging]]** for timely detection and accurate analysis.
    
- **Key Phases:** Includes Preparation, Detection & Analysis, **Containment** & Eradication, Recovery, and Lessons Learned.
    
- **Mandate:** A detailed **Playbook** must be established for common scenarios, defining the mandatory steps to isolate the attacker and secure the system.
