A **Data Retention Policy** is a mandatory organizational policy that outlines how long specific categories of information should be kept, how it should be stored during that time, and the secure, auditable method by which it must be destroyed when its retention period expires.

- **Goal:** To enforce **[[Data Minimization]]** by preventing the indefinite storage of sensitive or obsolete data. This reduces security liability and ensures compliance with laws like **[[GDPR]]** and **[[PCI DSS]]**.
    
- **Key Components:** A list of data categories (**[[Data Classification]]**), the required retention period (based on legal, regulatory, and business needs), and the approved method of **[[Data Sanitization]]**.
