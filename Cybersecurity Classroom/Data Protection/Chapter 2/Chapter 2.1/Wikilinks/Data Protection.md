**Data Protection** is the process of safeguarding sensitive information against corruption, compromise, or loss, and ensuring the rights of individuals regarding their **[[Personally Identifiable Information (PII)]]**.

- **Goal:** To enforce the **[[CIA Triad]]** (Confidentiality, Integrity, and **[[Availability]]**) throughout the entire data lifecycle (At Rest, In Transit, In Use) and comply with regulatory mandates (**[[GDPR]]**, **[[HIPAA]]**, **[[PCI DSS]]**).
    
- **Key Strategies:** Includes **[[Data Classification]]**, **[[Encryption]]**, **[[Access Control]]** (**[[Authorization]]**), **[[Key Management]]**, and implementing **[[Data Minimization]]** principles.
    
- **Context:** It is the overarching field that governs how organizations handle information to mitigate risk and meet legal obligations.
