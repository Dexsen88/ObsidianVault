**Non-Repudiation** is a security service that provides irrefutable proof of origin or delivery of data. It ensures that a party to a communication or transaction cannot successfully deny that they sent, received, or authorized the action.

- **Goal:** To enforce accountability and trust, particularly in financial and legal contexts.
    
- **Enforcement:** It is achieved using **[[Digital Signature]]**s. The unique, verifiable cryptographic link between a document and the sender's **[[Private Key]]** serves as irrefutable evidence, confirming both **Authenticity** and **[[Integrity]]**.
