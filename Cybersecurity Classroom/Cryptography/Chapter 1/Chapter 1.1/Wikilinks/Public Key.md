The **Public Key** is one half of the key pair used in **[[Asymmetric Encryption]]**. It is designed to be shared openly with anyone, similar to publishing a phone number.

- **Role in Encryption:** The **Public Key** is used by a sender (Aisha) to encrypt data intended for the key owner (Elena).
    
- **Security:** It is mathematically impossible (within reasonable time) to derive the matching secret **[[Private Key]]** from the public key.
    
- **Role in Signatures:** It is used to verify a **[[Digital Signature]]** created by the matching **[[Private Key]]**.
