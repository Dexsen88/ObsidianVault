**Encoding** is the process of transforming data into a format that can be reliably transmitted or stored across different systems or protocols (e.g., URL encoding, Base64 encoding).

- **Goal:** To ensure data integrity and reliable transmission by representing all characters in a common, safe format. It is used to overcome protocol limitations (e.g., reserved characters in URLs).
    
- **Mechanism:** It is a **two-way** processâ€”the data can be easily decoded back to its original form.
